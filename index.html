Designing at the edge of technology and people.

I hold an MSc in Interactive Arts and Technology from Simon Fraser University, with a foundation in architectural engineering. That combination shapes how I approach problems: I care about both the systems behind a product and the people who use it.

I work across the full design cycle — from research and whiteboard sessions through wireframing, prototyping, and developer handoff. I am most drawn to projects involving spatial interfaces, emerging technology, and real user complexity.
